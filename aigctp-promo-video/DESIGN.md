## Style Prompt

AIGCTP promo uses a cute, lively lifestyle-tech look: a bright morning canvas, rounded but tidy product surfaces, soft fruit and sky accents, tiny motion details, and friendly UI cards that feel useful rather than decorative. The video should feel like a cheerful assistant helping someone plan a day, not like a generic SaaS launch reel.

## Colors

- Canvas: `#fff8ed`
- Surface: `#ffffff`
- Text: `#27324a`
- Muted text: `#6f7a8f`
- Primary accent: `#3b82f6`
- Warm accent: `#ffb84d`
- Health accent: `#2fbf71`
- Food accent: `#ff6b7a`

## Typography

- Chinese UI and headings: `"Microsoft YaHei", "PingFang SC", "Noto Sans SC", sans-serif`
- Numeric and code-like labels: `"SF Mono", Consolas, monospace`

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use a dark cyberpunk or purple-blue neon palette.
- Do not make the story a static card grid.
- Do not use stock-photo placeholders or empty laptop mockups.
- Do not hide the product value behind abstract AI language.
- Do not make every element centered with equal visual weight.
